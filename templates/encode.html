Hide Message

To hide your message into an image, choose a photo from your device, enter your secret message, security key for the image and then click on Hide Message button.

Save the last image, it will contain your hidden message.

Remember, the more text you want to hide, the larger the image has to be. In case you choose an image that is too small to hold your message you will be informed.
